The Farm

It all begins high in the mountains, in the rich volcanic soil of the Bean Belt. Here, under the perfect balance of sun and shade, coffee plants flourish, waiting for the rainy season to blossom.

The Harvest

Once the coffee cherries ripen to a deep, vibrant red, they are carefully picked by hand. Only the finest cherries are selected to ensure the highest quality for the next stage of the journey.

The Roasting

This is where the magic happens. The green coffee beans are heated, transforming their chemistry to unlock the aromatic oils and rich flavors that we know and love. It's both an art and a science.
